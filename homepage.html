About Us

Welcome to "Zima's Gallery" the wackiest art gallery website founded by eccentric artists and tech-savvy jesters. Spilling paint on their keyboards during a paintball fight sparked this platform, showcasing avant-garde and humorous masterpieces. From "The Tribute Money" to "Metamorphosis of Narcissus" expect giggling emojis critiquing art and abstract tutorials by a virtual cat.
