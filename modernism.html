Modern Art

Modern art, spanning the late 19th to mid-20th century, represents a diverse range of artistic movements that challenged traditional norms.

It prioritized innovation, experimentation, and personal expression, often departing from realistic representation.

Embracing abstraction, symbolism, and conceptual exploration, modern art redefined artistic boundaries and reflected the evolving complexities of the modern world.
